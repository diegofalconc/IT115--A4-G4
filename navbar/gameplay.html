Gameplay

What's more fun than watching dogs? Washing dogs! In Wash Dogs we take dog washing to the next level, allowing you to enjoy the fun of washing dogs without the mess.

Adventure Mode:
Play as a motivated pet groomer who is just starting their dog washing business as the game takes you on an adventure through the world of competitive dog washing.

Multiplayer Mode:
Compete online with friends or players from around the world to find out who is the ultimate dog washer in a fast-paced and adrenaline-pumping head-to-head battle.

Key Features:
- Real voice acting from famous puppers from around the world.
- A selection of over 100 dogs and 25 breeds.
- Unlock different tools and shampoos to help you tackle the muddiest and dirtiest dogs.
- Compete with your friends and find out who is the best dog washer in a unique multiplayer experience.
